Flags

This game is based on a card game "Flags of the world". Your goal is to guess as many flags as possible.

To start with, each player receives 5 cards with flags of countries. At the beginning of each turn, player chooses a card with a flag
and makes a guess which country owns the flag. For every correct answer player receives 1 point. After each attempt players take turns.
After going through all of the 189 cards, player with the most points wins.

Note: Make sure that spelling of your entry is correct (input is not case sensitive).

Have fun!
